# Official policy baseline

Snapshot date: 2026-07-21. Refresh the applicable region before a review if the check date is more than three calendar days later.

## Cross-market baseline

Across TikTok Shop markets, illegal, stolen, counterfeit/gray-market, third-party-IP-infringing, sanctioned/illegally imported, recalled, and materially unsafe goods are prohibited. Platform eligibility also depends on category state: prohibited, unsupported, restricted, or invite-only.

IP protections cover products plus titles, images, descriptions, short video, LIVE, shop name, and avatar.
